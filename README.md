# Notes vault

This folder is an **Obsidian vault** organized around the **ACE framework** from Nick Milo ([Linking Your Thinking](https://www.linkingyourthinking.com/)). ACE is a note-taking and folder layout for Obsidian built on **links**, not only nested folders.

## What ACE is

**ACE** stands for **Atlas**, **Calendar**, and **Efforts** — three “headspaces” for how you work with knowledge:

| Headspace | Orienting lens | Purpose | Guiding question |
|-----------|----------------|---------|------------------|
| **Atlas** | Knowledge | Understand — ideas, concepts, Maps of Content (MOCs) | Where would you like to go? |
| **Calendar** | Time | Focus — daily notes, logs, reflections on a date | What’s on your mind? |
| **Efforts** | Action | Act — projects and work by importance / energy | What can you work on? |

Atlas is *space* (knowledge), Calendar is *time*, Efforts is *how much you’re investing* — together they cover both knowing and doing.

### Efforts vs “projects”

**Efforts** is deliberately not just “projects.” Efforts use **intensities** (e.g. On, Ongoing, Simmering, Sleeping) so you can throttle energy instead of treating everything as only active or inactive.

## This vault right now

| Location | Role in ACE / notes |
|----------|---------------------|
| **`atlas/`** | Atlas — e.g. `atlas/maps/` for topic maps and MOC-style notes. |
| **Root** (`*.md`) | Scratch notes, templates (e.g. `sample_template.md`), starters like `Welcome.md`. |
| **`.obsidian/`** | Obsidian settings (themes, plugins, daily-notes config, etc.). |

You can add **`calendar/`** and **`efforts/`** (or names you prefer) when you’re ready — ACE does not require every headspace on day one.

## Beginner tip (keep it simple)

1. **Knowledge bits** → Atlas  
2. **Daily / random capture** → Calendar (often daily notes)  
3. **Project / action notes** → Efforts  

Skip deep subfolders until your own structure feels necessary. A larger variant is **ACCESS** (Atlas, Calendar, Cards, Extras, Sources, Spaces) for more advanced vaults.

## Using Obsidian here

1. Open this folder in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) as a vault.  
2. Connect notes with `[[wikilinks]]` — that’s central to how ACE is meant to work.  
3. Change look and plugins in **Settings** (updates `.obsidian/`).
