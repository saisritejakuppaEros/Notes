## 💡 Study Tips

- **Daily target:** 1.5–2 hours per topic session
- **Revision days:** Use flashcards, draw diagrams from memory, summarize in your own words
- **Practice sessions:** Spend 10 min on requirements → 20 min on high-level design → 15 min on deep dive components
- **Tools to use:** Excalidraw or draw.io for diagrams, Notion/Obsidian for notes
- **Key resources:** ByteByteGo, System Design Primer (GitHub), Grokking the System Design Interview
